### Part B: The Content Waterfall System methodology
A real, well-designed content distribution framework. Strip the Matt Gray branding and what remains is:

- **One pillar piece** (long-form written content — for him X thread, but works for any 1500+ word source)
- **Atomic decomposition** — break the pillar into 10 standalone tweet-sized ideas
- **Format multiplication** — each idea becomes a short video script (~95 words = Reel/Short/TikTok), one carousel image, one LinkedIn riff
- **Time-shift redistribution** — same content reused over 4 months across surfaces
- **Cross-pollination** — short videos cross-post to X; long-form YouTube becomes newsletter; newsletter becomes carousel

**Leverage ratio:** 30:1. One pillar = 30 derivative posts.
